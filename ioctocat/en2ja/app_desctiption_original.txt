
---
iOctocat is a GitHub app for the iPhone and iPod Touch.

The application features:
* Feeds: News Feed and My Activity
* My Repositories: Private, Public and Watched
* Repository: Issues, Recent Commits, Commit Details, Network/Forks and Watching
* User Profile with Repositories and Following
* Search for Users and Repositories
